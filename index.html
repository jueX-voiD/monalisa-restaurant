ABOUT MONALISA

Monalisa Hospitality Group represents a commitment to authentic Nepali culture and exceptional dining experiences. Born from the vision of founder Usha Serchan, whose legendary Thakali cooking in the mountains of Mustang became the foundation of our first restaurant, we have grown into a collection of distinguished hospitality ventures across Nepal. Each brand under our umbrella shares the same unwavering dedication to authenticity, quality, and genuine warmth that made our flagship Monalisa Thakali Restaurant a celebrated destination.

We believe hospitality is more than service—it’s about preserving cultural heritage, creating memorable experiences, and welcoming guests not as customers, but as family. From traditional Thakali cuisine to diverse dining concepts, every Monalisa Hospitality location upholds our core values: sourcing authentic ingredients, honoring traditional methods, and delivering the warm, genuine hospitality that defines Nepali culture.
